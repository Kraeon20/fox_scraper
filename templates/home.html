About My Project

Exploring Data on Google Maps

Hey there!
So, I've been tinkering around with Google Maps lately, and I've come up with something pretty cool. You know how businesses are always looking for ways to connect with potential customers? Well, I thought, why not help them out a bit? This project basically dives into Google Maps and pulls out publicly available info like names, addresses, emails, phone numbers, and even social media profiles like Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Twitter. It's all about making those connections easier for everyone involved. And guess what? I built it using Python and Playwright.
It's been quite the journey, but I'm really proud of how it's turned out!
